Our Courses

This course introduces learners to the art of yarn crafting, including knitting, crochet, and weaving. By the end, participants will be able to identify different yarn types, master basic stitches, and complete a small project such as a scarf or decorative wall hanging. The course is divided into four modules: tools and materials, basic techniques, intermediate projects, and finishing touches. Learners will need yarn, needles, and scissors. The course runs for 6 weeks with weekly hands-on sessions.
